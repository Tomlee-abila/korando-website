ABOUT US

Together, We Can Build a Brighter Future for Every Child

At Dolfine Korando Faith Foundation, we believe in the power of community. By lifting each other up, we can provide the education, care, and resources that children need to thrive.

Since 1997, we have been dedicated to transforming the lives of vulnerable children and widows by providing free education, vocational training, healthcare, and emotional support. Our mission is to empower the next generation and create a sustainable, compassionate future for all.
